Lasting Album Remarks

Definetly a great album! Most recommended for R&B fans, the album really resonnates on a chill sort of lofi vibe, so if your looking for an album to listen to ambiently or an album to decompress this album is for you, or even if you just want something light and enjoyable its soft and delicate on the ears while having some catchy undertones that make you want to listen to over and over again without getting tired of it.
