The Artist: Jack Harlow

Jackman Thomas Harlow is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter from Louisville, Kentucky, who rose to fame with his 2020 hit "Whats Poppin." Signed to Generation Now/Atlantic, he has scored three Billboard Hot 100 number ones, including “First Class” and “Lovin on Me,” and earned major accolades like the 2021 Billboard Top New Artist award. His albums include That’s What They All Say, Come Home the Kids Miss You, and Jackman, and he made his acting debut in the 2023 White Men Can’t Jump remake.

About the Album:Monica

Monica is the third studio album by American rapper Jack Harlow. It was released on March 15, 2023, through Generation Now and Atlantic Records. The album features guest appearances from artists such as Lil Wayne, Ashanti, Megan Thee Stallion, and more. Monica received generally positive reviews from music critics and debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart.
